WHO WE ARE

ENET'COM Junior Entreprise

Founded in February 2012, ENET'Com Junior Entreprise is a student company with an economic and educational vocation, not-for-profit located at the National School of Electronics and Telecommunications of Sfax. Our club allows students to complement their theoretical training by confronting the professional world through a range of training courses and projects. Our club is part of the JET international movement, which today counts more than 38 junior companies -located in various prestigious schools- and more than 2,500 young Tunisian entrepreneurs.
